Balanced Nutrition

You don't have to eat less,
you have to eat right.

Follow the 3 month rule

If you genuinely want a strong, fit and amazing body in really less time, I have a tip for you all and it goes like this: Do not eat deep/shallow fried, added oil/sugar food and any sort of desert for 3 months. By doing this you will absolutely see quick results (considering you are following your [at least] 1 hour 30 minute workout schedule in a very disciplined manner). Here is a short list of the substances you have to avoid:

Vegetable Oils: These disrupt your body's omega 6-to-3 ratio (a high ratio can cause excess inflammation in the body and increase the risk of various diseases) and causes increase in overall bad cholesterol in the body.

Refined Carbs: Foods such as white bread, sodas, sweets, pastries are high in refined carbs and there over intake is linked to increase in obesity and metabolic diseases.

Trans Fats: Over consumption of bakes foods like cakes, cookies, pies, microwave popcorn, fries foods which are high in trans fats are which may cause serious health problems such as heart diseases in the long run.

Veggies Shall Be Your Buddies

• Focus on eating least/uncooked veggies i.e. salad if possible. This will bring two changes. Firstly, the nutrients are lost more and more the veggies are processed, you will get them all and then whatever added unhealthy products like oil, tastemakers and all, won't go inside your body.

• If you can't live on only salad, of course you can't, try to fill your dish with 25%-30% of chopped raw veggies like carrots, cucumber, spinach, radish, tomato and whatever you like but try to make your dish colourful. Lemon is also great for weight loss and you can squeeze its fresh juice on literally anything like onions, chopped salad, etc. Eating carrots is the best option.

• Salads are high in fiber which helps to reduce bad cholesterol. Fibers aid in digestion and helps in prevention of bowel diseases. Salads like spinach helps to build strong bones and improve the performance of energy producing structures inside our cell, i.e. mitochondria. High water levels in salad will increase the hydration in your bodies and thus improve your skin tone. Adding sprouts in the daily diet will turbo-charge the nutritional value in your bodies.

Increasing the Fruits Intake

Now you know what not you have to eat. So let's talk about the things you have to add/increase in your diet. Firstly fruits, discarding all those high fat containing snacks like pizzas, burgers, fries and all, you have to add highly nutritious and healthy fruits in your daily life.

The mechanism of this is like, instead of eating high (bad) calorie foods, healthy fruits go into your stomach. So, along with not feeling hungry anymore, you will consume something highly nutritious and less fat containing. Fruits like bananas, apples and oranges are high in fiber. Eating those in your late afternoon starving will keep you full for a long time.

Drinking fruit juices with no added sugars (watch it, I am not saying milkshakes) instead of coffee or tea is always better as unlike added sugars, you will consume natural or no sugars and you can have variety of juices, of single fruit or a mixed fruit juice.
