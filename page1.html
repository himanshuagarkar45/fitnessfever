Elementary Level

To start with your physical training, do a short workout daily in the morning or evening. Other thing which has to be followed is jogging (or running if possible) daily, following the 10/2 rule.

I have explained the jogging/running process broadly in my Intermediate part. Now as you know about your physical training, we will focus on your diet, go to Nutrition Segment to know more. 

Quote by Banksy

Quote by Megan Shaeff

To make most out of a run or a jog, you can use 10/2 Rule in which you have to jog for the maximum time you can and then once you have totally fatigued yourself, you have to slow down and start brisk walking till it feels like you can start the jog again.
Here, 10 denotes a 10 minute run/jog and 2 denotes a 2 minutes brisk walk which you have to do alternately.

Intermediate Level

  The more the days you keep yourself physically active, the more your body will accept your lifestyle change and then, you can easily convert those short time duration workouts into the longer ones.

Workout applicaions (available to Play Store and App Store) which will be focusing more on your specific body parts. Consider a certain app has a leg workout course of 5 months, so once you complete this one, you can shift to another app focusing on your arms or chest.

Along with 30 minutes sports and workout each, you have to give time for a 30 minutes run daily, which you will be doing according to the 10/2 Rule
